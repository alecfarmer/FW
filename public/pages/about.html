About

BaseShowcase was created to help players and coaches get a head start on the recruiting process. Since recruiting is difficult to do one your own, we try to help by providing an intuitive and simple way to get yourself out there. We strive to get every registered player a chance at playing at the next level. We provide both player and coach profiles where information is provided on height, weight, stance, throwing side, SAT score, and more. We hope to make this process simple and easy so the focus is on improving skills on the field while your profile does the rest.

Level Age Years of Experience

There are many levels of play during a players career. We tend to focus on the earlier and middle years of play as that is when the player is developing their skills. Starting at middle school age, some players possess the skills to play at a higher level and want to showcase their abilities to next level recruits.
